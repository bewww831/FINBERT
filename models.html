Weight Justification

Why each model contributes the amount it does to the final score

XGBoost 52.5%
FinBERT 32.5%
ResNet-18 15.0%
📈 XGBoost 52.5%

Highest weight due to its 95% holdout accuracy and strong backtesting performance. Price action and technical indicators are the most direct signal for short-term directional movement.

📰 FinBERT 32.5%

Second highest weight reflecting 89.4% accuracy and 87.4% macro F1. News sentiment is a well-established driver of short-term price moves, making it a strong complementary signal to price data.

📊 ResNet-18 15.0%

Lowest weight as chart pattern recognition is the least precise of the three at 71.7% accuracy. It still adds value as an independent visual signal — particularly useful when XGBoost and FinBERT conflict.
